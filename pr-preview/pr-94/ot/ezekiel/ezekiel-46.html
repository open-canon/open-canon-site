4 And the burnt offering that the prince shall offer unto the Lord in the asabbath day shall be six lambs without blemish, and a ram without blemish.

10 And the prince in the midst of them, when they go in, shall go in; and when they go forth, shall go forth.
11 And in the feasts and in the asolemnities the meat offering shall be an ephah to a bullock, and an ephah to a ram, and to the lambs as he is able to give, and an hin of oil to an ephah.
