Chronicles — Chapter 3

David’s sons are named—The successors of Solomon to Jeconiah and beyond are listed.

1 Now these were the asons of David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess:
1 The third, aAbsalom the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, bAdonijah the son of Haggith:
1 The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife.
1 These six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he areigned seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years.
1 And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and aSolomon, four, of bBath-shua the daughter of cAmmiel:
1 Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,
1 And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,
1 And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.
1 These were all the sons of David, beside the sons of the aconcubines, and bTamar their sister.
1 ¶ And Solomon’s son wasaRehoboam, Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,
1 Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,
1 Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,
1 Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,
1 Amon his son, Josiah his son.
1 And the sons of Josiah were, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third aZedekiah, the fourth bShallum.
1 And the sons of aJehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.
1 ¶ And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son,
1 Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.
1 And the sons of Pedaiah were,aZerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister:
1 And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab-hesed, five.
1 And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah.
1 And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.
1 And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.
1 And the sons of Elioenai were, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven.
